Carolina Herrera

Logistics leader

Carolina Herrera is an open-minded person, passionate about acquiring new knowledge, she is a problem solver, she likes to challenge herself and meet the objectives she sets out in the most efficient way possible; qualities that in Vinix Code she has had the opportunity to apply and polish in her position as Logistics Leader, where she is in charge of supervising the optimal operation of the processes in the company and establishing action plans to meet the goals proposed in each project.

She studied English in Malta during a semester at London School of Commerce, where she lived in a multicultural environment and acquired a passion for languages. She is currently completing her university studies in Environmental Engineering, a degree that combines her love for the environment and the exact sciences. Besides, she is studying a Bachelor in Foreign Languages ​​with an emphasis in English, and she has experience teaching in the mathematics field.

Her hobbies are reading, painting and enjoying nature camping or hiking. She loves math, logic, art, crafts, caring for the environment, traveling, cultural exchanges, languages, and teaching.
