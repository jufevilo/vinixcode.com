Katheryne Castro

Administrative Manager

Katheryne Castro is the Administrative Manager of Vinix Code S.A.S in Colombia. Katheryne has extensive knowledge of administrative operations, human talent, and the Joint Committee on Occupational Health at the company. Her experience also includes caring for people in basic assistance and medicalized transportation, customer service, processing and recording information through computer files and databases, and the writing of letters and administrative reports; which compose a solid base for the achievement of the organizational objectives by taking care and supplying the needs of the collaborators for the professional and personal growth of each one of them.

Katheryne has more than 5 years of experience in customer care in the health area and brings this experience to each department of the organization to take care of the physical and mental health of the workers.

Katheryne has a Professional Technique in Prehospital Care and a Technology in Business Administration and Finance. She is currently finishing her university studies in Business and Financial Administration. Katheryne enjoys taking care of people (especially children and the elderly), being at family gatherings, cooking, throwing birthday parties, reading books about interpersonal relationships, traveling, and listening to music.
